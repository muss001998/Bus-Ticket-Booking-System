The Bus Booking Project

The Bus Booking Project was inspired by my own experiences navigating public transportation as a student. Growing up in a large city, I often relied on buses to get me to school, work, and social events. However, I frequently found the process of booking and managing bus tickets to be cumbersome and frustrating. Long lines at the station, lack of real-time updates, and limited booking options made it difficult to plan my trips efficiently.

Recognizing this pain point, I decided to take on the Bus Booking Project as part of my portfolio work at Holberton School. The goal was to create a user-friendly platform that would streamline the bus booking process and provide commuters with a more seamless transportation experience. Over the course of several months, my team and I worked diligently to design, develop, and test the application, drawing on our skills in full-stack web development, UX/UI design, and project management.

This project has been an invaluable learning experience, allowing me to apply the technical and collaborative skills I've developed at Holberton. It has also given me the opportunity to make a tangible impact on the lives of fellow commuters, which is something I'm deeply passionate about.
